# Žižkov Tunnel Association – Web Project

This repository contains the source code for the web presentation of the Žižkov Tunnel Association (Spolek žižkovského tunelu). This is an independent initiative founded in 2025 by prominent figures from the fields of architecture, art, and culture.

## Project Vision and Goals
* **Space Transformation:** The goal is to transform the Žižkov Tunnel into a vibrant gallery of contemporary art and an open public space.
* **Connecting Residents:** The project aims to bridge the gap between the city and its inhabitants.
* **Cultural Axis:** The tunnel serves as a symbolic space for encounters and shared experiences.
* **Urban Districts:** The project connects the districts of Žižkov, Karlín, and Holešovice.
* **Inspiration:** The concept is inspired by world-renowned projects such as the High Line in New York City.
* **Values:** The association is built on principles of openness, collaboration, and long-term care for public space.

## Founding Members
The project is backed by a team with extensive expertise:
* **Martin Wichterle:** Industrialist, owner of the Wikov engineering group and the Bomma and Rückl glassworks.
* **Jiří Řezák:** Architect and owner of the QARTA Architektura studio.
* **David Koller:** Musician, producer, and cultural initiator.
* **Jiří Fajt:** Art historian and artistic director, former Director General of the National Gallery in Prague.

## Concept and Implementation
* **Long-term Plan:** The project envisions a gradual transformation of the tunnel tube over a ten-year period.
* **Regular Installations:** Three works by Czech and international artists will be installed annually.
* **Site-specific Approach:** Individual installations are designed specifically for the tunnel space, reflecting its unique atmosphere.
* **Preserving Functionality:** Installations must fully respect safety conditions and allow for the fluid movement of pedestrians/cyclists.
* **Entrance Areas:** The project includes the cultivation of tunnel entrances, creating zones for exhibition openings and social gatherings.
* **Curatorial Oversight:** The selection of artworks is conducted under the leadership of Jiří Fajt and other experts.

## Technical Instructions for AI (Cursor)
When generating code and website content, adhere to the following context:
1. **Strategic Significance:** The tunnel connects key cultural landmarks such as DOX, the Holešovice Market, the future Vltava Philharmonic, and Dům Radost.
2. **Atmosphere:** The website should reflect the contrast between the tunnel's utilitarian transport function and its high cultural potential.
3. **Prestige:** The project is presented as a long-term investment in the international prestige of Prague.
4. **Partnership:** The association works in close cooperation with the City of Prague and the relevant municipal districts.
